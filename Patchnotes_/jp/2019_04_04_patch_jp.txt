##Continuous Content
###1. Limited Event "Ink-Stained Steel Sakura" Rerun
  
<ul> 
  
<li>Event period: March 28th, 2019, after maintenance to April 18th, 2019, maintenance.

</ul>
★<strong>How To Play</strong>
<li>The following ships will be available in certain event maps as drops in both normal and hardmode:

<ul>
<ul>
<li>SSR  <strong>Kawakaze</strong> (DD)
<li>SR  <strong>Kongou</strong> (BC)
<li>R  <strong>Ooshio</strong> (DD)
</ul> 
</ul>

<ul>
<li><strong>Sakura Amulets</strong> can be obtained by clearing event stages and completing limited event missions..
<li>Sakura Amulets can be utilized in following ways:
<ul>
<li>Sakura Amulets can be exchanged for rewards such as SR Kongou, SR Haruna, and R Ooshio in the event shop.
<li>By reaching certain milestones of total Sakura Amulets obtained, you can earn rewards such as SR Mutsu. To check your event progress, please tap on the event banner on the home screen.
</ul>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Bonus element <strong>"Special Target Capture"</strong>
<ul>
<li>After the flagship fleet has been destroyed, a "Siren Transport Fleet" has a chance to appear. The Siren Transport Fleet will try to escape out through the map's edge. The Siren Transport Fleet must be captured by surrounding it on all 4 sides using the following:
<ul>
<li>a. Ally fleets
<li>b. Physical obstructions on the map
<li>c. Placeable and retrievable sea mines
</ul>
<li>Bonus Sakura Amulets can be obtained by successfully capturing the Siren Transport Fleet.
<li>In the case that you retreat from the stage and the Siren Transport Fleet has not been captured, no bonus Sakura Amulets will be rewarded.
<li>In the case that you fail to capture the Siren Transport Fleet and it is able to escape from the map, a small amount of Sakura Amulets will still be rewarded.
</ul>
</ul>
</ul>
</ul>

<li>There will be a challenging EX stage to test your skills.
<li>You will not gain items, ship drops, or experience in the EX stage.
<li>EX stage does not consume fuel or morale.

★<strong>Notice</strong>
<ul>
<li>Normal stages will reward 3x Sakura Amulets once daily.
<li>The event will implement the Danger Gauge system
<li>The event maps will have no ambushes, airstrikes, or submarine enemies</li>
<li>You can exchange Sakura Amulets until April 24th, 23:59 JST.
</ul>
</ul>
