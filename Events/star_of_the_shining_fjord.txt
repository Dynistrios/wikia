### Event Map

##### â˜…<strong>How To Play</strong>


<ul>
<li>Bonus element <strong>"Coastal Battery"</strong> may appear in some event maps.
When an ally fleet (other than a submarine fleet) enters the range of <strong>"Coastal Battery"</strong> (red area), it will be bombarded by the coastal batteries and the movement speed will decrease. In addition, a new enemy alert fleet may appear in the map.
<li><strong>"Coastal Battery"</strong> can be destroyed by friendly fleets by getting beside them.
<li>Bonus element <strong>"Encirclement Mode"</strong> will be present.
<li>After 3 staring and achieving "Safe" Danger Gauge rating in events maps the player can select this mode in the map selection screen; the following changes will take place
<ul>
<li>Enemy placement-behavior logic changes.
<li>You can move freely in the map without tile restrictions
<li>Enemy coastal batteries will no longer appear.
</ul>
</ul>
